Giving Back

Lovable GoldStar Puppies is proud to support the larger community and ecosystem through education, responsible breeding, and dedicated partnerships.

Canine Care Certified™ (CCC)

We proudly support Canine Care Certified™, a nationwide Purdue University program promoting the highest standards of dog welfare and responsible breeding.

Lovable GoldStar Puppies is proud to have 64 Canine Care Certified™ breeders and more than a dozen applicants currently pending certification.

The Indiana Council For Animal Welfare (ICAW)

Lovable GoldStar Puppies is an active member of the Indiana Council for Animal Welfare (ICAW), helping to advance animal care and responsible breeding practices throughout Indiana.

  • Members since 2018
  • Annual sponsor of the Southern and Northern ICAW Seminars
  • Currently serving on the Southern Indiana ICAW Board

The Luv Pet Project

We’ve proudly supported The Luv Pet Project since 2020 — a compassionate initiative that promotes responsible pet ownership, helps fund rescue efforts, and uplifts local animal welfare programs.
