About Us

We are located in Montgomery, Indiana and provide services throughout the continental United States

"At Lovable Gold Star Puppies, our team is dedicated to elevating the standards of the pet industry through ethical business practices and compassionate care. With a focus on creating a nurturing environment for our furry friends, we've become a beacon for responsible pet ownership. Our mission intertwines with my personal values, fostering a culture where every team member is empowered to contribute to the welfare of the animals and the satisfaction of our clients. As the steward of this vision, I'm committed to leading our organization to new heights, ensuring that we continue to deliver love and joy to families, one puppy at a time."
-- President Nathan Waglner
